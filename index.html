🌍 Renewable Energy System Designs

☀️ Activity 1: Rooftop Solar PV System (5 kW)
  • 🔆 Source of energy: Solar irradiance captured by rooftop photovoltaic modules rated at 5 kW. Panels convert sunlight directly into DC electricity using the photovoltaic effect.
  • ⚙️ Conversion process: DC from panels passes through DC combiners and MPPT charge controllers to maximize efficiency. The inverter converts DC to grid-synchronized AC. Li-ion batteries store energy for night use or outages.
  • 🏡 Output / utilization: Supplies lighting, appliances, and critical loads. Excess power exported through net metering.
  • 🌿 Real-world relevance: Reduces bills, enhances energy independence, and promotes distributed clean generation.
  • 🛠️ Design notes: Proper string sizing, grounding, and protective isolation are essential for safety and longevity.
🌤️ Activity 2: Hybrid Solar–Wind System for Rural Health Clinic
  • 💨 Source of energy: A combination of solar and wind ensures reliable 24/7 renewable power generation for critical healthcare facilities.
  • ⚙️ Conversion process: Solar PV produces DC, while wind turbines generate variable AC that is rectified and managed via a hybrid controller. The inverter and battery system stabilize output for essential medical loads.
  • 🏥 Output / utilization: Provides uninterrupted power for vaccine refrigeration, lighting, and diagnostic equipment.
  • 🌍 Real-world relevance: Enhances rural healthcare reliability while reducing fossil-fuel dependency.
  • 📐 Design notes: Includes battery autonomy analysis, surge protection, and remote monitoring for performance tracking.
🌊 Activity 3: Ocean Thermal Energy Conversion (OTEC) System
  • 🌡️ Source of energy: Utilizes temperature difference between warm surface water and cold deep seawater in tropical regions.
  • ⚙️ Conversion process: Warm seawater heats a working fluid in an evaporator. The vapor drives a turbine generator, and cold seawater condenses the vapor back, completing the cycle.
  • 🔋 Output / utilization: Generates clean baseload electricity; byproducts include desalinated water and cooling for aquaculture.
  • 🌍 Real-world relevance: Provides sustainable coastal energy, fresh water, and cooling applications in tropical regions.
  • 🧠 Design notes: Focus on efficient heat exchangers, corrosion control, and eco-friendly seawater intake design.
♻️ Activity 4: Biogas Power Generation System
  • 💩 Source of energy: Organic waste such as cow dung, crop residues, and food waste undergo anaerobic digestion producing methane-rich biogas.
  • ⚙️ Conversion process: Methane gas is collected and burned in a biogas engine generator to produce electricity; digestate slurry is used as fertilizer.
  • 🔋 Output / utilization: Powers rural homes, farms, or small industries, providing both energy and soil nutrients.
  • 🌿 Real-world relevance: Converts waste to wealth, reduces greenhouse emissions, and supports sustainable rural economies.
  • 📐 Design notes: Maintain digester temperature (30–35°C), proper feedstock ratio, and ensure leak-proof construction.
